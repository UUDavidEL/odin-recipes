Homemade Horseradish

Wilma's Homemade Horseradish

This is a modern horseradish recipe that uses a food processor to make this fiery condiment. It tastes fresher and is much more pungent that store-bought.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peeled and cubed horseradish root
  • 3/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine horseradish, vinegar, sugar, and salt in the bowl of a food processor or blender; pulse until blended. Carefully remove the cover of the processor or blender, keeping your face away from the container.
  2. Cover and store horseradish in the refridgerator for up to one month.
